CONSULTING AGREEMENT

This Consulting Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on August 10, 2024,
between Strategic Advisors LLC ("Consultant") and StartupVentures Inc. ("Company").

CONSULTING SERVICES:
Consultant will provide strategic business advisory services including:
- Market analysis and competitive positioning
- Business model optimization  
- Fundraising strategy and investor relations
- Operational efficiency improvements

ENGAGEMENT TERMS:
- Engagement Period: 12 months
- Monthly Retainer: $15,000
- Success Bonus: $25,000 upon Series A funding completion
- Time Commitment: Minimum 20 hours per month

COMMUNICATION:
Consultant: strategy@strategicadvisors.com, admin@strategicadvisors.com  
Company: ceo@startupventures.com, operations@startupventures.com

DELIVERABLES AND MILESTONES:
Q1: Market Research Report and Competitive Analysis
Q2: Business Model Refinement and Revenue Optimization Plan
Q3: Fundraising Strategy and Investor Pitch Deck
Q4: Operational Process Documentation and Scaling Plan

EQUITY COMPENSATION:
In addition to monetary compensation, Company grants Consultant 0.5% equity
in the form of restricted stock units vesting over 2 years.

CONFIDENTIALITY AND NON-COMPETE:
Consultant agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of Company's business 
information, customer lists, and proprietary technology. Non-compete clause
applies for 1 year in the same industry vertical.

EXPENSES:
Company will reimburse reasonable business expenses including travel,
accommodation, and meeting costs with prior approval and proper documentation.

INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS:
Consultant is an independent contractor and not an employee. Consultant is
responsible for own taxes, insurance, and business expenses unless specifically
agreed to be reimbursed.

TERMINATION PROVISIONS:
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.
Company may terminate immediately for breach of confidentiality or non-performance.
Upon termination, all confidential information must be returned or destroyed.

DISPUTE RESOLUTION:
Any disputes will be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with
the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of New York, United States.
